Frequently Asked Questions

Find a list of the most frequently asked questions about $ETHSHIB.

What slippage do I need to use?

The transaction fee for a buy is 14% and sell is 17%. When buying through PancakeSwap, you may need to increase the slippage higher.

How many tokens do I need to receive $ETH rewards?

You need a minimum of 200,000 $ETHSHIB to earn $ETH rewards.

How will I receive my $ETH rewards?
Rewards are delivered to your wallet automatically once the threshold of earnings is reached. Rewards are not pegged to a specific time so you can receive them at any point. If trading volume is high, you will receive more $ETH more frequently.
How do I see my $ETH rewards?
To see your $ETH rewards in your wallet, you may need to add the Binance-pegged $ETH contract to your wallet.
Is the liquidity locked?

Yes. Liquidity is locked for 5 years.

Will the contract be audited?

Yes. Our contract is audited by Coinscope.

Are there any manual burns planned?
Not unless announced. We reserve manual burns for strategic times in order to protect our investors from price manipulation.
Is there a maximum holding amount?

Yes. 10 Trillion tokens will be the maximum per wallet (1% max wallet).
